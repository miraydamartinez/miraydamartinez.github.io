About me

Mirayda Martinez is a second-year student at Swarthmore College pursuing a double major in Economics and Computer Science. In 2015, she interned at Plains All American, a midstream energy company that specializes in the production of crude oil. Her responsibilities at Plains ranged from passing journal entries in Oracle database applications for accruals in monthly, quarterly, and yearly close to preparing and processing invoices and monthly management reports.

In 2017, she served as the Manager for the Swarthmore Student Budget Committee (SBC), which allocates approximately $600,000 in funds to student groups.

Following her position as Manager, Mirayda completed a week-long software engineering externship at Salesforce, an American cloud computing company that specializes as a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform. During her externship, she gained familiarity with Kubernetes, an open-source system used to automate deployment, scale, and manage containerized applications.

Mirayda shares an interest for social entrepreneurship, startups, machine learning, deep learning, and A.I. She is currently looking to pursue opportunities at the intersection of computer science, economics, and mathematics.
